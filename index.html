Stand For Trees

Stand For Trees is an action campaign that uses the power of social media and crowd-funding to fight global deforestation. Stand For Trees puts the power to save forests in the hands of the people to whom the future matters most: young people. It allows everyday citizens to crowd-fund a more sustainable future for local and indigenous communities while conserving forests, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and protecting wildlife.
